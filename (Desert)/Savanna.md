# Savanna
- Acacia, Baobab, and Borassus Trees
- Tumbleweeds

## Variants

### Wooded
Has many more trees and shrubs, providing a lot more cover and shade
### Shrubby
Doesn't have many trees, but has lots of shrubs and bushes
### Grassy
Is completely lacking in trees and shrubs, covered only with grasses. It's incredibly similar to [Prairies](./Prairie.md), actually, and can easily transition into them.
